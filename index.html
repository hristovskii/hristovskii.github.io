About me

Greetings! I am a 20-year-old, currently immersed in the dynamic realm of Computer Science and Engineering. Hailing from the vibrant capital city of Skopje in Macedonia, I am on a journey to unravel the mysteries of technology and innovation. My love for programming was ignited at a young age, and now I am channeling this passion into my studies, exploring the depths of algorithms, data structures, and software development. Beyond the screen, I am an avid sports enthusiast, finding joy in the rush of the game and the camaraderie it fosters. Equally, I lose myself in the world of books, relishing diverse stories that broaden my horizons. Balancing the virtual world with the real, I find solace in video games—a realm where creativity and strategy intertwine. A remarkable sense of humor is my constant companion, an attribute that infuses positivity into interactions. And when it's time to unwind, I appreciate the simple pleasure of a well-crafted beer. Life's journey is a fusion of bytes and moments, and I am here to embrace both with open arms. Join me as I code my dreams, laugh heartily, and toast to the beauty of life—one line of code and one sip of beer at a time.

Reading books

Escaping into the world of books has become a cherished hobby, offering me a retreat from the mundane and a portal to endless adventures of the mind. Among my favorite authors, Agatha Christie reigns supreme. Her detective thrillers have an uncanny ability to grip me, the intricate plots and skillfully woven mysteries leaving me spellbound. The way she crafts her characters and plots is nothing short of mesmerizing. Diving into the pages of a Christie novel is akin to embarking on a mental puzzle-solving journey. Beyond Christie, I've found myself engrossed in the psycho thrillers penned by Sebastian Ficek. The psychological depths he explores and the eerie atmospheres he creates are both captivating and spine-chilling.

Sports

Sports have ingrained themselves deeply in my life, infusing it with exhilaration and vivid memories. Martial arts, particularly kickboxing and Muay Thai, have not just shaped my physique but also cultivated discipline and determination within me, evident in the medals I've earned.
